Our Story

From the Groves
of Greece to Your Table

Olio Deli was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to connect Athens food lovers with the finest Greek producers. Every product on our shelves has been personally selected by our team, visiting farms, dairies, and workshops across Greece.

We are located at Efronios 22, Pangrati - a short walk from Syntagma Square - and we ship across Greece and Europe.
