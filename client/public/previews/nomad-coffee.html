Our Story

Rooted in Craft, Guided by Curiosity

Nomad Coffee Co. was born from a simple belief: that great coffee deserves to be understood, not just consumed. We source directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships that ensure quality and fairness at every step.

Our roastery in Limassol is open to visitors. Come see how we work, taste the difference between origins, and take home a bag of something extraordinary.
