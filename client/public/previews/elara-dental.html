About Dr. Elara

Dentistry Built on Trust

Dr. Elara Charalambous graduated from the University of Athens School of Dentistry and completed her postgraduate training in implantology and cosmetic dentistry in London.

Since returning to Limassol in 2010, she has built a practice known for its calm atmosphere, transparent pricing, and exceptional clinical outcomes.
