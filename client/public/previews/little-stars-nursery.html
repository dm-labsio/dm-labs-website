About Us

A Place Where Children
Feel at Home

Founded in 2012, Little Stars Nursery has been a trusted part of the Chalandri community for over a decade. Our team of qualified early years educators creates a warm, stimulating environment where every child is valued as an individual.

We are located at Agias Paraskevis 14, Chalandri, Athens - with easy access from Kifissia, Marousi, and central Athens.
