# D&M Studio — Design Brainstorm

## Brief
International small-business website studio. Mobile-first, conversion-focused. Target: non-technical small business owners. Primary CTA: WhatsApp. Tone: friendly, professional, reassuring.

## Idea A — "Editorial Calm" (Modern European SaaS)

**Design Movement:** Swiss Modernism meets contemporary SaaS editorial (Stripe / Linear.app)

**Core Principles:**
- Radical whitespace as a luxury signal
- Asymmetric left-anchored text blocks with right-side visual weight
- Typographic hierarchy does the heavy lifting — no decorative elements
- Every section breathes; nothing competes

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #F6F6F4 (warm off-white — feels premium, not sterile)
- Text: #111315 (near-black charcoal — authority without harshness)
- Accent: #4C7DFF (professional blue — trust, technology, clarity)
- Muted: #8A8F98 (cool grey for secondary text)
- Palette of 3 only — discipline communicates confidence

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Left-anchored headings with generous right margin
- Sections alternate between full-bleed light and subtle warm-grey backgrounds
- Cards with 1px border + soft shadow (no heavy borders)
- Process steps as horizontal timeline on desktop, vertical on mobile

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule separators (1px, low opacity)
- Small monochrome line icons (24px, 1.5px stroke)
- Subtle blue accent dot / underline on active states

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover: subtle background shift + slight translateY(-2px) on cards
- Buttons: smooth background transition 200ms
- Navigation: underline slide-in on hover

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up (opacity 0→1, translateY 20px→0) over 400ms, staggered per section
- No parallax, no bounce
- WhatsApp button: gentle pulse ring on idle

**Typography System:**
- Display/H1: Plus Jakarta Sans 700, 42–56px, tight letter-spacing
- H2: Plus Jakarta Sans 600, 28–36px
- Body: Inter 400, 16–18px, line-height 1.7
- Caption/label: Inter 500, 13px, uppercase, 0.08em letter-spacing

## Idea C — "Confident Minimal" (Selected)

**Design Movement:** Modern European SaaS + editorial calm — inspired by Framer, Stripe, Linear.app

**Core Principles:**
- Whitespace as the primary design element
- Typography-first hierarchy — text carries the message, visuals support
- Consistent 8px spacing system throughout
- Mobile-first: every layout decision starts at 375px

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #F6F6F4 (warm white — premium, not cold)
- Text: #111315 (deep charcoal — authority)
- Accent: #4C7DFF (professional blue — trust, action)
- Hover accent: #3A6AE8 (slightly darker blue)
- Muted text: #6B7280 (grey — secondary information)
- Card background: #FFFFFF with subtle shadow
- Palette strictly 4 colors — discipline = confidence

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Left-anchored content blocks, NOT centered
- Hero: headline left, device mockup right (stacked on mobile)
- Alternating section backgrounds (warm white / pure white)
- Process: horizontal numbered steps with connecting line
- Pricing: two cards side by side, Business Website subtly highlighted

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin 1px accent-blue left border on feature callouts
- Small checkmark icons (✓) in accent blue for trust signals
- Subtle gradient blob in hero background (very low opacity, brand colors)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover: card shadow deepens + translateY(-3px)
- Buttons: 200ms ease transition on background/border
- WhatsApp float: pulse animation ring every 4s

**Animation:**
- Section entrance: fade-up 400ms, 60ms stagger between items
- No parallax, no bounce, no rotation
- Performance priority — all animations use transform/opacity only

**Typography System:**
- H1: Plus Jakarta Sans 700, 40–52px desktop / 32–38px mobile
- H2: Plus Jakarta Sans 600, 28–34px
- H3: Plus Jakarta Sans 600, 20–22px
- Body: Inter 400, 16–18px, line-height 1.7
- Small/label: Inter 500, 12–13px, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.06em

## Selected Design: **Idea C — "Confident Minimal"**

This approach best matches the brief: modern European SaaS feeling, mobile-first, conversion-focused, non-technical audience. The warm white background with charcoal text and blue accent creates immediate trust. Left-anchored layouts avoid the "AI template" look. Typography-first hierarchy ensures clarity on small screens.
